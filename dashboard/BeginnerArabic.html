Beginner Arabic Course

Start your journey to learning Arabic with our comprehensive beginner course. Designed for absolute beginners.

Course Overview

This Beginner Arabic course introduces you to the basics of the Arabic language. You will learn essential vocabulary, basic grammar, and conversational skills to start speaking Arabic confidently.

  • Introduction to Arabic script and pronunciation
  • Basic vocabulary and phrases
  • Simple sentence structure and grammar rules
  • Listening and speaking practice through interactive exercises

Course Outline

Week 1: Introduction to Arabic Script

Learn the Arabic alphabet, basic pronunciation, and common greetings.
Week 2: Essential Vocabulary

Expand your vocabulary with essential words and phrases used in everyday conversations.
Week 3: Basic Grammar and Sentence Structure

Understand fundamental grammar rules and practice forming simple sentences.
Week 4: Introduction to Conversations

Engage in basic conversations, ask and answer questions, and discuss simple topics.
Week 5: Listening and Speaking Practice

Enhance your listening skills and practice speaking through interactive exercises and role-plays.
Week 6: Review and Assessment

Review all topics covered in the course and assess your progress with quizzes and exercises.
